Notes:

  • Catchment delineation for groundwater is subject to significant uncertainty. e.g. Waimakariri numerical modelling with calibration-constrained Monte Carlo uncertainty analysis yielded potential catchment areas 10 x greater than the area of land required to provide the rate of water abstraction from a community supply well

  • There are multiple active projects (inc GNS MBIE TW project) looking at catchment area delineation - the outputs of this work could be linked to here and method recommendations made
